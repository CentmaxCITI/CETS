About C.E.T.S

Centmax Engineering and Technology Services has been in operation since January 2017 providing consultancy services in the fields of ground water resources, piped water engineering design, geospatial (GIS) technology, environmental management, general engineering and WASH consultancy. The company has been mainly involved with private sector, central and local governments, NGOs and Civil Society. We also provide human capacity development in the areas of ground water resource management, Engineering Design, ICT and Project Management through our partner institute; Centmax Institute of Industrial Training-CITI. A list of our training programs can be accessed via our website: citi.ac.ug. We therefore invest in research, technology and innovation by developing and retaining the best talent in the company for consultancy services.

Our E-learning atmosphere provides a lively, interactive and practical self paced learning with a student and tutor community of over 2000. You can therefore be assured of an enriching and immersive learning atmosphere with state-of-art visual aids that are recommended by Academia and Industry. We also have a thriving community of alumni across a range of sectors from Engineering, ICT, Environment, Business and Finance to name a few.
